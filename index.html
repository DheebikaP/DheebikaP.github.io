About Me

I am a PhD Scholar at the Indian Institute of Technology Madras (Thesis Submitted). My research interests lie at the intersection of memory studies, affective embodiment, and South Asian literature. Currently, I serve as the Graduate Student Representative for the South Asian Literary Association (SALA).
